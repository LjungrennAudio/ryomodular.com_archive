OPTODIST

LjunggrenAudio RYO Optodist: an OptoVCA with distortion and LED limiting.

- Distortion with characterful overdriven sound.
- VCA. - on board LED limiter circuit soft-clips signal through 4 LEDs on front panel for a different flavour of distortion.
- Basic manual attenuator.
- Analogue optocouplers used for Non-intrusive voltage control of VCA.
- All analogue circuitry.
- Hi-fidelity signal chain with low noise opamps.
- Buffered inputs and outputs.
- Sim 6HP footprint.
- Compact 31mm Depth (with power cable attached).
- Low power draw: 16mA+12V / 16mA -12V /no +5V required.
- Works for audio as well as CV.
- Plucky dynamic CV response.
